Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is a symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar, located in Delhi, is the tallest brick minaret in the world. It is an important historical landmark, with intricate carvings and inscriptions from the 12th century.

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India, located in Mumbai, was built in 1924. It was the ceremonial entrance for British governors and is now a major tourist attraction.
